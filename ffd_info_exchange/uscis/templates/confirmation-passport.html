Thank you for applying for a U.S. passport — your confirmation number is XC451N.

Once your Application for Naturalization is approved, the Department of State will review your passport application. A representative from the Department of State will contact you directly regarding the status of your application; processing usually takes four to six weeks.

The Department of State will keep your information on file for two years, after which they will securely remove it from their records.

Questions? Call the Department of State’s service center at 1-800-975-3000.
